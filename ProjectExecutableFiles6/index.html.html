What Is Fetal AI

FetalAI is a cutting-edge technology that leverages artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning algorithms to analyze and interpret fetal health data, providing insights and predictions for better prenatal care. FetalAI combines advanced imaging techniques, such as ultrasound and MRI, with AI-powered analysis to:Enhance fetal imaging: Improve image resolution and clarity for more accurate assessments.Predict fetal growth: Estimate fetal weight and growth rate, abnormalities. Monitor fetal well-being: Track fetal heart rate, movement, and other vital signs. Provide personalized care: Offer tailored recommendations for pregnancy management. FetalAI aims to revolutionize prenatal care, enabling healthcare providers to make more informed decisions and improve fetal health outcomes. Reduction of child mortality is reflected in several of the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals and is a key indicator of human progress.
